# Operations Handbook

## SLOs / SLAs
- Define SLOs per service (availability, latency, error rate).
- SLA commitments documented per plan.

## On-Call Rotation
- Primary + secondary rotation
- Escalation policy for SEV1/SEV2

## Incident Management
- Standard incident workflow
- Post-incident review within 5 business days

## Disaster Recovery
- Quarterly DR tests
- Documented RTO/RPO targets

## Capacity Planning
- Quarterly capacity reviews
- Load testing prior to major releases

## Release Management
- Change approvals
- Rollback playbooks
- Feature flags by plan
