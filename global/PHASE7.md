# Phase 7: Global Expansion & Regulation Readiness

## 1) Strategic Architecture
- Regional data planes with sovereign storage.
- Policy-enforced data residency routing.
- Cross-border governance models with regional policy overlays.

## 2) Organizational Design
- Global compliance ops
- Regional data protection officers
- Regulatory engagement team

## 3) System Requirements
- GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, FedRAMP, PCI-DSS aligned controls
- Regional audit trails and access segregation

## 4) Risk Register
- Cross-border data leakage
- Divergent regional regulatory requirements

## 5) Execution Roadmap (3–5 years)
- Year 1: regional readiness for US/EU
- Year 2: APAC + Middle East compliance
- Year 3–5: global audit pipeline maturity

## 6) Capital Requirements Estimate
- $10M–$25M for compliance operations and regional infra
