# Phase 9: Industry Standardization & Ecosystem Control

## 1) Strategic Architecture
- Open protocol specs with reference implementations
- Certification pipeline for partner integrations
- Governance of standards through partner councils

## 2) Organizational Design
- Standards committee
- Partner governance team
- Open source program office

## 3) System Requirements
- Versioned protocols and schemas
- Certification test suites
- Reference implementations for key verticals

## 4) Risk Register
- Fragmented ecosystem standards
- Partner non-compliance
- Reputation risk from weak certification

## 5) Execution Roadmap (3–5 years)
- Year 1: draft protocols + reference implementation
- Year 2: certification program launch
- Year 3–5: multi-vertical standards adoption

## 6) Capital Requirements Estimate
- $5M–$15M for standards leadership and developer advocacy
